About the Game

Project Dex is an exhilarating VR movement game inspired by the hit game Gorilla Tag. Use your hands to climb, push off surfaces, and navigate vibrant environments with physics-based movement that feels natural and incredibly fun.

Built from the ground up for VR, Project Dex delivers an active, social experience where movement is everything. No teleportation, no artificial locomotion—just pure, skill-based gameplay that will get your heart racing!

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Project Dex respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play Project Dex.

Information We Collect

a. Voice Data

Project Dex accesses your device's microphone to enable real-time voice communication during gameplay. Voice data is processed instantly and is not stored on our servers or recorded by the app. Please note, however, that other players may hear your voice during active sessions. Always use this feature responsibly and be mindful of the information you share verbally.

b. Device Information

We may collect certain information about the device you use to play the Game, including but not limited to your device's model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

c. Usage Data

We collect data on how you interact with the Game, such as gameplay statistics, in-game actions, and the amount of time spent playing. This data helps us improve the Game and provide a better user experience.

d. Log Data

When you access the Game, our servers automatically record certain information, including your IP address, game session details, and the time and date of your interaction with the Game. This data is used for security purposes and to help us understand how the Game is used.

e. Data Usage Check-Up

We periodically check the data usage of players to ensure efficient operation and to help players monitor how much data is consumed while playing the game. This includes monitoring network data and server requests during gameplay. We do not collect personal information during this check-up, but data on overall usage patterns helps us optimize performance and server capacity.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Game: Your voice data and usage data help us ensure the Game functions as intended and allows for player communication.
  • To improve the Game: We analyze device information, usage data, log data, and data usage check-up results to enhance gameplay features and fix issues.
  • To protect our users: We may use log data, device information, and data usage metrics to monitor and prevent fraudulent activity or unauthorized access.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Game, conducting our business, or providing services to you, provided that these parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

Security of Your Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Project Dex is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us.

Your Rights and Choices

All users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may also have other rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access or correct the data we have about you. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
